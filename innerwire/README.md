## What is InnerWire?

InnerWire is an open model of individual differences based on verified scientific theories from neurobiology, psychology, and physiology. Unlike popular typologies (MBTI, Socionics, Human Design), InnerWire doesn't offer pseudoscientific "types." Instead, it provides a profile across several axes, each backed by peer-reviewed research.

*Inner ‚Äî internal. Wire ‚Äî wiring. Your inner wiring: the nervous system, perception, and response patterns that make you who you are.*
