## What is this

InnerWire is a model of individual differences based on measurable parameters of the nervous system, perception, and behavior. Unlike popular typologies (MBTI, Socionics, Human Design), InnerWire doesn't offer pseudoscientific "types." Instead, it provides a profile across several axes, each backed by peer-reviewed research.

## Who it's for

- People who want to understand themselves but can't bring themselves to believe in astrology
- People who value an evidence base and want to see references
- People looking for practical tools, not just descriptions
- Professionals who need an integrative framework

## Principles

1. **Scientific foundation** ‚Äî every claim is backed by references to peer-reviewed research
2. **Layers of complexity** ‚Äî each layer is self-contained and useful on its own
3. **Spectrums, not types** ‚Äî people vary along continuums, not discrete categories
4. **Dynamics** ‚Äî your profile isn't a verdict; it changes with state, context, and practice
5. **Openness** ‚Äî the model is open to criticism, contribution, and evolution
