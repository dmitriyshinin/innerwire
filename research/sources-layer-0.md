# Sources / Источники: Autonomic Profile / Автономный профиль

## Key works / Ключевые работы

### Polyvagal Theory / Поливагальная теория

- **Porges, S. W. (2011).** *The Polyvagal Theory: Neurophysiological Foundations of Emotions, Attachment, Communication, and Self-Regulation.* W. W. Norton & Company.
  — The foundational monograph. Full exposition of the theory.
  — Основополагающая монография. Полное изложение теории.

- **Porges, S. W. (2007).** The polyvagal perspective. *Biological Psychology, 74*(2), 116–143.
  — Review article, good for a first encounter with the theory.

- **Porges, S. W. (2003).** Social engagement and attachment: A phylogenetic perspective. *Annals of the New York Academy of Sciences, 1008*(1), 31–47.

- **Dana, D. (2018).** *The Polyvagal Theory in Therapy: Engaging the Rhythm of Regulation.* W. W. Norton & Company.
  — Practical application. Most accessible introduction.
  — Практическое применение. Наиболее доступное изложение.

### Criticism / Критика

- **Grossman, P., & Taylor, E. W. (2007).** Toward understanding respiratory sinus arrhythmia: Relations to cardiac vagal tone, evolution and biobehavioral functions. *Biological Psychology, 74*(2), 263–285.

- **Taylor, E. W., Wang, T., & Leite, C. A. C. (2022).** An overview of the phylogeny of cardiorespiratory control in vertebrates with some reflections on the 'Polyvagal Theory'. *Biological Psychology, 172*, 108382.

### Heart Rate Variability (HRV) / Вариабельность сердечного ритма

- **Thayer, J. F., & Lane, R. D. (2009).** Claude Bernard and the heart–brain connection: Further elaboration of a model of neurovisceral integration. *Neuroscience & Biobehavioral Reviews, 33*(2), 81–88.

- **Laborde, S., Mosley, E., & Thayer, J. F. (2017).** Heart rate variability and cardiac vagal tone in psychophysiological research – Recommendations for experiment planning, data analysis, and data reporting. *Frontiers in Psychology, 8*, 213.

- **Beauchaine, T. P. (2015).** Respiratory sinus arrhythmia: A transdiagnostic biomarker of emotion dysregulation and psychopathology. *Current Opinion in Psychology, 3*, 43–47.

### Breathing practices / Дыхательные практики

- **Zaccaro, A., Piarulli, A., Laurino, M., et al. (2018).** How breath-control can change your life: A systematic review on psycho-physiological correlates of slow breathing. *Frontiers in Human Neuroscience, 12*, 353.

- **Balban, M. Y., Neri, E., Kogon, M. M., et al. (2023).** Brief structured respiration practices enhance mood and reduce physiological arousal. *Cell Reports Medicine, 4*(1), 100895.

### Stress cycle / Стрессовый цикл

- **Nagoski, E., & Nagoski, A. (2019).** *Burnout: The Secret to Unlocking the Stress Cycle.* Ballantine Books.

### Somatic approaches / Соматические подходы

- **Levine, P. A. (2010).** *In an Unspoken Voice: How the Body Releases Trauma and Restores Goodness.* North Atlantic Books.

- **Ogden, P., Minton, K., & Pain, C. (2006).** *Trauma and the Body: A Sensorimotor Approach to Psychotherapy.* W. W. Norton & Company.

### Neuroception / Нейроцепция

- **Porges, S. W. (2004).** Neuroception: A subconscious system for detecting threats and safety. *Zero to Three, 24*(5), 19–24.
