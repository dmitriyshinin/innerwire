# Quick Profile

Your full picture in 20 minutes.

InnerWire maps five aspects of how you function: your nervous system's default mode, how well you sense your own body, how you process sensory input, how you build closeness with others, and how much stress your body is carrying right now.

This is the fast version. No theory, no jargon — just honest questions and recognizable patterns. Answer from your real experience over the last few months, not from how you'd like things to be.

## How it works

1. **[Take the questionnaire](questionnaire.md)** — 5 short sections, ~15 minutes total
2. **Read your results** — each section ends with a brief description of what your answers suggest
3. **[Pick starter practices](practices.md)** — 2–3 concrete things you can try this week

## What this is not

This is not a personality type. Your profile is not fixed — it changes with your circumstances, health, relationships, and deliberate practice. It's a snapshot of where you are now, not a sentence of who you are.
